Coaches

Meet our coaches…

Coach Bell, Coach Hardie, Coach Evans, Coach Andriano, Coach McFadyen

John Evans

Varsity Head Coach

Coach Evans joins us for his fifth year as Head Coach and his sixth overall with the softball program. Before joining the softball program he was a member of the Walton Football staff where he coached the offensive line and ran the Strength and Conditioning program. Coach Evans is also the Head Boys Tennis Coach and has been for the past 15 seasons. Before coming to Walton, Coach Evans was on the football staff at East Hall High School in Gainesville, GA where he also graduated from and lettered in football, wrestling, and baseball. He has been at Walton for 19 years. Coach Evans has three children: Preston, Mollie, and Peyton.

You can e-mail him at: John.Evans@cobbk12.org

Emily Andriano

JV Head/Varsity Assistant Coach

Coach Andriano brings more than 8 years of experience with her to the Walton team. She has taken the fields as a competitive travel ball player, Walton high school varsity starter, defensive player of the year award recipient, and travel ball assistant coach. She is excited to begin her career as a math teacher at Walton and continue coaching as part of Walton’s Fastpitch team.

Mike Bell

JV Assistant/Varsity Assistant Coach

Coach Bell brings years of coaching and playing experience to the JV Softball Program. As a coach, he brings years of experience from several competitive softball organizations. As a player, he played on several high school championship baseball teams and lettered in football. Coach Bell is a certified Team USA Softball Coach and excited to support the development of the JV Softball program.

Marah Hardie

Varsity Assistant Coach

Coach Hardie is a third year biology teacher at Walton. She graduated from Kennesaw State University in 2016 with a Bachelor of Science Degree and a Masters of Art in Teaching Science Degree in 2020. She is the sponsor for Rally Club and the assistant coach for the Varsity Girls Soccer team at Walton. Marah has five years of coaching experience, and is excited for her third season as a Raider!

Shane McFadyen

Varsity Assistant Coach

Coach McFadyen is a first year social studies teacher at Walton. He brings 18 years of coaching experience to the Raider program. Coach McFadyen has served as a varsity head softball, baseball and basketball coach, as well as an assistant football coach at previous schools. He graduated from Hillsdale College (MI) in 1997 with a Bachelor of Science Degree in Finance, where he was a four year letter winner for the Charger baseball team. Coach McFadyen is married to Rebecca McFadyen and they have three daughters.

Home games: 1590 Bill Murdock Rd, Marietta, GA 30062
